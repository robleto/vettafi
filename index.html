Passive has its perks… and its drawbacks. 

 

As an advisor, you know that a passive, market-tracking index ETF can often be the most prudent investment vehicle for clients.

 

But you also understand the apprehension your clients might feel being tied to a market-tracking index like the S&P 500, especially when fears of recession loom large. 

 

What if you could offer your clients the low cost and convenience of a passive ETF, but also the potential opportunity that comes from high-conviction stocks handpicked by professional analysts? 

Introducing TMFC
The Motley Fool 100 Index ETF

TMFC is the only ETF on the market that grants access to the 100 highest-rated, active stock recommendations from the analysts at The Motley Fool, LLC – all in a single passive ETF.

The best of both worlds 

TMFC combines the convenience and low cost of a passive ETF with the advantages of professional research and analysis. 

This gives your clients the opportunity to outperform the market, without the high fees of active management, or the difficult work of building a stock portfolio from scratch.
